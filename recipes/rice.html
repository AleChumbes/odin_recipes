Rice

It is basically rice cooked in various herbs and spices such as onion and garlic powder, paprika, and thyme.

An east rice dish that is super customizable according to your plate. to put it simply, you are just adding seasonings to plain rice to make it savory and flavorful.

It is almost the same with fried rice, except fried rice is cooked rice sauteed with various spices. In contrast, seasoned rice is uncooked rice along with spices.

Ingridients

  • Butter
  • Uncooked Long Grain White Rice
  • Salt
  • Oregano, Thyme, and Parsley
  • Paprika
  • Pepper Flakes
  • Bouillon

Steps

  1. PUlle all the ingridients in a large pot and git it a stir.
  2. Place on the stove and bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Turn the heat to low. As soon as it starts to bubble, cover ans reduce to low heat.
  4. Let it simmer. Simmer for about 18-20 minutes or until all the liquid is fully absorbed.
  5. Turn off the heat. Let it rest for about 5 minutes, add another tablespoon of butter, if desired, then fluff with a fork and serve.
